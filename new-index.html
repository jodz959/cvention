What is CODEVENTION?

CODEVENTION exists to uncover innovative ideas from otherwise unreachable places. Instead of focusing mainly on the best minds that are already known, we want to seek out talent from unknown sources. Our HackShop Competition is designed to do exactly this. CODEVENTION seeks to engage with the technology and entrepreneurial community at large to foster collaboration, networking and an exchange of ideas. Our aim is to highlight both present and future leaders whose visions are rooted in the development of Jamaica and the Caribbean at large.

CODEVENTION is also concerned with supporting sustainable and scalable technology solutions. How can ideas be maintained? How can we reach a global market while having our businesses locally? Is it possible to develop solutions that apply to the Caribbean and thereby similar regions and countries? CODEVENTION seeks to explore the solutions to these questions through intellectual discussions with those who have navigated that space and now lead companies with a global market reach.

FAQ

Can anybody attend CODEVENTION?

Anyone who attends college or university in Jamaica or the Caribbean is encouraged to apply. A significant amount of applicants will be selected to attend for free, afterwhich a small number of tickets will go on sale. Applicants on the waitlist will be offered a discount.

A limited amount of tickets will be available for professionals and company representatives who are not sponsors at the event.

Where will the event be held?

The conference will be held in Kingston, Jamaica on Novemeber 29-30th. HackShop will occur on November 29th and CODEVENTION will occur on November 30th. The event location and travel information will be communicated directly to the attendees.

Can high school students attend?

Yes. 2 students from each high school across Jamaica are encouraged to apply. Only 25 will be selected to attend.

Can anybody attend HackShop?

Anyone who attends college or university in Jamaica or the Caribbean is encouraged to apply if they already have a team. Only 9 teams will be selected to participate in this event.

Hackshop is exclusive to the 9 teams, professional mentors and the judges.

How will I know I was selected?

You will receive an email from the team at CODEVENTION confirming your acceptance. You will be required to RSVP to confirm your place, failure to do so will result in your spot being given to someone on the waitlist.

When will the applications open?

The applications will be opened in May and we will accept responses on a rolling basis.
